Den Chief Finder
The Den Chief Finder helps Troops and Packs find each other to provide and make use of Den Chiefs.
  • For Troops:
    • Register your available Den Chiefs
    • Search for Cub Scout Dens looking for Den Chiefs in your area and see Den and Pack meeting times and places

  • For Cub Scout Dens:
    • Register your Den that is looking for a Den Chief
    • Search for Troops with available Den Chiefs in your area

    Note! Please make sure to adhere to youth protection guidelines - you may only post contact information for adults to facilitate communication, not the contact information for youth.
